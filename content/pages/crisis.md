# Crisis Resources

These resources are free. Available 24/7. No insurance needed. No appointment needed. Right now.

---

## Immediate Help

### Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
**Call [988](tel:988)** — 24/7, free, confidential

Available for anyone in suicidal crisis or emotional distress. Connects you with a trained counselor. Available in English and Spanish. TTY users can call 711 then 988.

### Crisis Text Line
**Text HOME to [741741](sms:741741)** — 24/7, free, confidential

Text-based crisis support. You'll be connected with a trained crisis counselor. Available in English, Spanish, and French.

### Veterans Crisis Line
**Call [988](tel:988), press 1** — 24/7, free, confidential

Specifically for veterans, service members, and their families. You can also text 838255 or chat online at VeteransCrisisLine.net.

### Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ Youth)
**Call [1-866-488-7386](tel:18664887386)** — 24/7, free, confidential

Crisis intervention and suicide prevention for LGBTQ+ young people. Also available via text (text START to 678-678) and chat at TheTrevorProject.org.

## Specific Situations

### Domestic Violence
**National Domestic Violence Hotline: [1-800-799-7233](tel:18007997233)**
Text START to 88788. Available 24/7. Safety planning, resources, and support.

### Substance Abuse
**SAMHSA National Helpline: [1-800-662-4357](tel:18006624357)**
Free, confidential, 24/7 treatment referral and information service. Available in English and Spanish.

### Sexual Assault
**RAINN National Sexual Assault Hotline: [1-800-656-4673](tel:18006564673)**
Free, confidential, 24/7. Also available online at rainn.org.

### Child Abuse
**Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline: [1-800-422-4453](tel:18004224453)**
Available 24/7 in over 170 languages.

### Financial Crisis
**211** — Dial 2-1-1 for local assistance with food, housing, utilities, and other essential services.
