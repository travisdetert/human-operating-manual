# About My Contract

---

## What This Is

My Contract is a personal operations framework. It borrows from systems engineering, DevOps, behavioral science, and financial planning to create a comprehensive methodology for managing your life.

It is not self-help. It is not motivational. It does not promise transformation through mindset shifts, vision boards, or morning routines designed by millionaires.

It is a diagnostic and engineering approach: audit your current state, identify failures, build systems, execute in sprint cycles, review with data, iterate.

---

## What Makes It Different

**It's free.** All 27 modules of the core methodology — every word — are free and will stay free. The self-help industry charges you for information. We charge for tools that help you implement the information.

**It's complete.** Most frameworks cover one slice of life (money, habits, fitness, productivity). My Contract covers finances, physical health, habits, time management, relationships, career, mental health, and the meta-system that connects them all.

**It has population calibrations.** The same framework, tuned for veterans in transition, neurodivergent individuals, people re-entering society after incarceration, those living with depression, couples, parents, and people in crisis. Because "one size fits all" is another lie the industry tells.

**It's data-driven.** Every claim is backed by cited research or verifiable statistics. Every tool produces numbers you can track. The methodology doesn't ask you to believe — it asks you to measure.

**It doesn't moralize.** Your financial situation, your weight, your habits — these aren't moral failures. They're system failures. And systems can be redesigned.

---

## The Model

**Free tier:** All 27 modules of content + 5 calculators (habit cost, compound interest, time remaining, real hourly rate, credit card payoff).

**Premium tools:** Interactive tools for building and tracking your contract — Contract Builder, Sprint Tracker, Honest Audit Tool, Weekly Review Dashboard. Your data stays in your browser (LocalStorage). We never see it.

---

## The Mission

Build the framework that the self-help industry should have built — and give it away.

Charge for tools that make implementation easier, not for the knowledge itself.

Serve populations that the wellness industry ignores or exploits.

Replace motivation with engineering. Replace inspiration with arithmetic. Replace guru culture with systems thinking.
