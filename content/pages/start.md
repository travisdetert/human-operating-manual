# Start Here

### Do something today. Not tomorrow. Not Monday. Today.

---

## Quick Start (15 Minutes)

1. **Pull up last month's bank statement.** Right now. On your phone is fine.
2. **Write down three numbers:** Income $\_\_\_\_\_\_ | Spending $\_\_\_\_\_\_ | Difference $\_\_\_\_\_\_
3. **Pick 3 contract terms** from the starter list below.
4. **Sign it.** Put it where you'll see it daily.
5. **Set a weekly review:** every Sunday, 15 minutes.

That's it. That's the beginning. You can read the [full methodology](/methodology/manifesto/) later. But do these five things first.

---

## Starter Contract Terms (Pick 3)

### Financial
- [ ] Track every dollar I spend for 30 days
- [ ] No purchase over $50 without 24-hour wait
- [ ] Cancel \_\_\_\_\_ unused subscriptions this week
- [ ] Auto-transfer $\_\_\_\_\_ to savings on payday
- [ ] Pack lunch \_\_\_\_\_ days per week

### Physical
- [ ] Walk/move for 20 min, \_\_\_\_\_ days per week
- [ ] In bed, screens off, by \_\_\_\_\_ on weeknights
- [ ] Drink \_\_\_\_\_ oz of water daily
- [ ] Schedule overdue \_\_\_\_\_ appointment

### Habit
- [ ] No phone for first 30 min after waking
- [ ] Screen time limit: \_\_\_\_\_ hours/day on non-work apps
- [ ] No DoorDash/UberEats for \_\_\_\_\_ days
- [ ] Replace \_\_\_\_\_ with \_\_\_\_\_ (one swap, specific)

---

## The Rules

1. **Start with 3 terms.** Not 10. Not 7. Three.
2. **Make them specific.** "Exercise more" is useless. "Walk 20 minutes, Mon/Wed/Fri" is a contract term.
3. **Make them measurable.** If you can't answer "did I do this today?" with yes or no, rewrite it.
4. **Review weekly.** Sunday evening, 15 minutes. Check every term. Honest count.
5. **Run 4-week sprints.** After 4 weeks, review results. Adjust terms. Start next sprint.
