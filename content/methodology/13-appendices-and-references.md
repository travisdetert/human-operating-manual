## Appendix G: Quick Start Guide

**If you've read everything and feel overwhelmed, do only this:**

### Day 1 (30 minutes):
1. Pull up your bank statement from last month
2. Write down your total income and total spending
3. Write down whether you had a surplus or deficit

### Day 2 (15 minutes):
4. Write a contract with exactly 3 terms:
   - One financial: "I will track every purchase for 30 days"
   - One physical: "I will walk for 20 minutes, 3 days this week"
   - One structural: "I will do a 10-minute weekly review every Sunday at [time]"
5. Sign it. Put it on your fridge.

### Day 3-30:
6. Follow the 3 terms. That's it. Nothing else.
7. Do your weekly review on Sundays.

### Day 31:
8. Review your first sprint. What was your compliance rate?
9. Based on what you learned, write Version 1.1 of your contract
10. Continue.

**That's the entire quick start. 3 terms. 1 sprint. 1 review cycle. Everything else builds from here.**

## Appendix H: Recommended Resources

### Books (Actually Useful, Not Fluff)

**Financial:**
- *The Simple Path to Wealth* -- JL Collins (investing fundamentals, clear and direct)
- *I Will Teach You to Be Rich* -- Ramit Sethi (automating finances, practical systems)
- *The Total Money Makeover* -- Dave Ramsey (debt elimination, baby steps -- ignore the investing advice)
- *Your Money or Your Life* -- Vicki Robin (reframing money as life energy)

**Habits & Behavior:**
- *Atomic Habits* -- James Clear (the best book on habit formation, period)
- *The Power of Habit* -- Charles Duhigg (the science behind habit loops)
- *Thinking, Fast and Slow* -- Daniel Kahneman (cognitive biases, how your brain actually works)

**Health:**
- *Why We Sleep* -- Matthew Walker (sleep science that will change your relationship with sleep)
- *The Obesity Code* -- Jason Fung (understanding insulin and weight regulation)
- *Spark* -- John Ratey (the neuroscience of exercise)

**Mental Health:**
- *Feeling Good* -- David Burns (the original CBT self-help book, genuinely effective)
- *The Body Keeps the Score* -- Bessel van der Kolk (trauma and the nervous system)
- *Running on Empty* -- Jonice Webb (childhood emotional neglect)

**Career:**
- *So Good They Can't Ignore You* -- Cal Newport (skill capital over passion)
- *Never Split the Difference* -- Chris Voss (negotiation)
- *Deep Work* -- Cal Newport (focused skill development)

### Free Tools and Resources

**Financial:**
- Mint / YNAB / EveryDollar (budgeting apps)
- AnnualCreditReport.com (free credit reports, legally mandated)
- 211.org / dial 211 (local assistance for housing, food, utilities, healthcare)
- IRS Free File (free tax filing for income under $79,000)

**Health:**
- MyFitnessPal (calorie/nutrition tracking, free tier)
- Couch to 5K app (beginner running program)
- YouTube (unlimited free workout videos)
- GoodRx (prescription drug price comparison and coupons)

**Mental Health:**
- 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call/text 988)
- SAMHSA Helpline (1-800-662-4357)
- Open Path Collective (reduced-cost therapy)
- 7 Cups (free online emotional support)

**Career:**
- freeCodeCamp, The Odin Project (free coding education)
- Coursera, edX (free audit of university courses)
- LinkedIn Learning (often free through public libraries)
- Your local public library (free access to books, courses, databases, workshops)
