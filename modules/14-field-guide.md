# The Field Guide

### Print This. Tape It to Your Fridge. Do the Work.

## SIDE 1: THE ESSENTIALS

### Quick Start (Do This Today)

1. Pull up last month's bank statement
2. Write down: **Income $______ | Spending $______ | Difference $______**
3. Pick 3 contract terms from the starter list below
4. Sign it. Put it where you'll see it daily
5. Weekly review: every Sunday, 15 minutes

### Starter Contract Terms (Pick 3)

**Financial:**
- [ ] Track every dollar I spend for 30 days
- [ ] No purchase over $50 without 24-hour wait
- [ ] Cancel _____ unused subscriptions this week
- [ ] Auto-transfer $_____ to savings on payday
- [ ] Pack lunch _____ days per week

**Physical:**
- [ ] Walk/move for 20 min, _____ days per week
- [ ] In bed, screens off, by _______ on weeknights
- [ ] Drink _____ oz of water daily
- [ ] Schedule overdue _____________ appointment

**Mental/Structural:**
- [ ] 15-minute weekly review every Sunday at _______
- [ ] Daily reboot: 5 min check-in (mood, stress, one priority)
- [ ] No phone first/last 30 min of day
- [ ] Screen time daily limit: _____ hours

### The Rules

- **Never miss two days in a row** (one day off is rest; two is a new habit)
- **Breach protocol:** Name it. Find the trigger. One countermeasure. Move on. No guilt spiral.
- **Sprint cycle:** 4 weeks. Plan > Execute > Review > Adjust.
- **Compliance target:** 75%+ is solid. Below 60% = reduce scope, don't "try harder."
